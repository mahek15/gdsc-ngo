Our Vision
Empowering people by promoting human dignity, generating hope and light for all.
Our Mission
We strive to empower the people of Khar Danda in building networks, promoting values, preserving family bonds, enhancing the quality of life through, holistic and sustainable development programs, with a specific focus on health care, women and child empowerment, education, self-reliance and leadership.
